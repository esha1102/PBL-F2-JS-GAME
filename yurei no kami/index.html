Game Controls

Use A & D or Arrow Keys to move LEFT and RIGHT.

Press Space to become a square.

Press S to become a rectangle.

Press W to become a circle.

×

Game Rules

1. Escape the urban legends in the fastest time.

2. Avoid obstacles and enemies along the way.

3. If you fail to collect all 10 orbs, you lose.

4. You can change form to get through the obstacles. -circle, square and rectangle-

Forms

1. Circle- Stick to the ceiling to avoid below obstacles.

2. Square- Normal form.

3. Rectangle- Get through narrow spots.
